A few Gender Identites
Identites Description
Cisgender Identifying with the gender one was assigned at birth
Transgender (Trans*) A gender identity that is different for the one that was assigned at brith
Genderqueer Existing outside of the binary of man and woman
Non-binary Existing outside of the of binary man and woman
Agender Not having a specfic gender identity
Bigender Having two genders existing together
Demigender Having an internal feeling about a particular gender
Gender Fluid Moving between genders
Intergender A mix between masculine and feminine idenitites
Pandgender Experiencing all genders
